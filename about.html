About

Chaminda Perera

Photographer · Sri Lanka

Dedicated to creative exploration.

I'm Chaminda Perera, a photographer based in Sri Lanka. My work moves between landscape, nature, wildlife and the night sky — wherever the light, the weather and a little patience lead me.

Photography, for me, is less about a single perfect frame and more about an experience worth standing inside. I'm drawn to quiet, cinematic moments: the last glow on a ridgeline, a single tree against a sea of stars, a coastline seen from far above.

Beyond personal work, I create images for brands and publications, produce fine art prints, and share what I learn through writing and workshops. If our visions align, I'd love to make something together.
