How galleries work

Photos and videos are organised as folders. To add or remove media:

  1. Drop image files into the matching photos_* folder (or make a new photos_Something folder). Videos go in Video/; print samples in an optional prints/ folder.
  2. Run python3 generate_manifest.py in the project folder.
  3. Refresh the site — galleries, the hero, the In Motion reel and the print samples all update automatically.
Video orientation (portrait vs landscape) is detected automatically. New photos_* folders appear as new series with no extra setup.

Where messages go

The contact form is wired to Netlify Forms. Every enquiry is captured in your Netlify dashboard under Forms → contact — no email address is exposed anywhere on the site.

  • Open your site in Netlify → Forms to read and export submissions.
  • To get enquiries emailed to you, add a notification under Forms → Settings & notifications (use your new domain email once it's ready).
When you publish on a host with built-in forms (e.g. Netlify Forms) or add a small backend, messages can be stored in a database and listed right here. See DASHBOARD.md.
